Ragdoll

Nature:Ragdolls are known for their laid-back and docile nature. They are affectionate and enjoy being held and cuddled.

Expertise:Ragdolls are excellent indoor companions and adapt well to indoor living. They are not particularly active and prefer to follow their owners around the house

Coat:Ragdolls have semi-long, silky coats that are easy to groom. Their coats come in various colors and patterns, with blue eyes being a defining trait.
