Maine Coon

Nature : Maine Coons are gentle giants known for their friendly and sociable personalities. They are often described as "dog-like" because of their loyalty and affection towards their owners.

Expertise : Maine Coons are skilled hunters and excellent mousers. They are also known for their intelligence and can be trained to walk on a leash or perform tricks

Coat : Maine Coons have long, thick, water-repellent coats with a shaggy appearance. They come in a variety of colors and patterns.
